== Attention is the binding constraint

Compute can be provisioned. Repositories can be replicated. AI inference can be scaled.

*Expert judgment, working memory, and sustained focus cannot.*

* A specialist can be employed full-time and still unavailable to a change
* Calendar capacity is not uninterrupted capacity
* A fatigued review is a review in name only

[quote]
____
The goal is not to maximize visible activity — it is to maximize valuable outcomes per unit of scarce human attention, while preserving quality, learning, and sustainability.
____

// ============================================================================

== Cognitive burden is multidimensional

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| Intrinsic | reasoning demanded by the problem itself
| Representational | difficulty of how the system is _expressed_
| Navigational | finding code, owners, tests, context
| Coordination | tracking people, approvals, sequencing
| Temporal | delays, interruptions, stale assumptions
| Verification | proving the change is correct and safe
| Tool interaction | IDEs, CI, review tools, AI assistants
|===

Interventions act on _different_ dimensions. One score erases the distinctions that make interventions possible.

== A cognitive work model

Human cognitive work on an Engineering Change latexmath:[$e$]:

[stem]
++++
W_{human}(e) = W_{create} + W_{understand} + W_{verify} + W_{coordinate} + W_{recover} + W_{decide}
++++

* All six terms are *latent* — none observable directly at enterprise scale
* Measurement becomes a problem of *inference and validation*, not counting

// ============================================================================

== Measuring latent burden through proxies

Useful non-invasive signals include:

* materially distinct files and concepts revisited
* time from opening a review to first substantive action
* repeated navigation through the same regions
* review-response cycles and unresolved threads
* simultaneously active Engineering Changes
* repository and domain transitions
* repeated CI runs without meaningful change
* AI findings requiring independent investigation

[.lead]
No single proxy is a "cognitive-load score." Combine, control, and validate.

// ============================================================================

== Context switching and attention residue

image::figures/mermaid/context-switch-cycle.png[Context switching and recovery cycle]

Recovery time model:

[stem]
++++
T_{recover}(p,e) = \alpha + \beta_1 \Delta t + \beta_2 N_{intervening} + \beta_3 C_e + \beta_4 D_e + \epsilon
++++

The shaded region under the recovery curve is *attention debt* — cumulative work-below-steady-state imposed by the interruption.

// ============================================================================

== Review is cognitive work, not elapsed time

Review is a sequence of operations:

. construct a mental model of intent
. identify changed behavior
. connect to architecture and domain constraints
. search for omissions and failure modes
. judge test evidence
. communicate findings actionably
. verify responses and revisions

A *1,000-line generated client* may require less reasoning than a *5-line authorization change*.

// ============================================================================

== Comment counts are weak measures

More comments can mean: careful review, poor code, stylistic noise, misunderstanding, or high consequence.

Fewer comments can mean: excellent code, superficial review, high trust, or fatigue.

Comment data becomes useful only when enriched:

* type · severity · novelty · action · resolution
* human- or AI-originated · evidence supplied
* eventual link to rework, defect, or incident

// ============================================================================

== AI as a cognitive participant

[IMPORTANT]
====
*Cognitive offload* — human work avoided because a reliable AI contribution performed or prepared it.

*Cognitive reload* — additional human work created by inspecting, correcting, rejecting, or recovering from AI output.
====

Net cognitive effect:

[stem]
++++
NCE(e) = W_{offload}(e) - W_{reload}(e)
++++

Positive = net offload. Negative = the AI increased burden.

// ============================================================================

== Why acceptance rate is insufficient

A suggestion may be accepted because it is trivial, auto-applicable, socially persuasive, or too hard to evaluate.

A valuable finding may be *rejected* because the author solved it differently.

Acceptance telemetry should be a _distribution_:

* direct application · application with modification
* independent equivalent fix · follow-up issue
* acknowledgement without change · rejection with reason
* no observable response · duplicate of human or AI finding

// ============================================================================

== The Cognitive Offload Index

[stem]
++++
COI = \frac{\hat{W}_{human,control} - \hat{W}_{human,treatment}}{\hat{W}_{human,control}}
++++

Quality-adjusted form:

[stem]
++++
QCOI = COI \times Q_r
++++

* latexmath:[$COI = 0.15$] means estimated human work fell 15% vs. counterfactual
* Never report without confidence intervals and guardrails
* *Never* rank individuals or unrelated teams

// ============================================================================

== Instrumenting AI review interactions

image::figures/mermaid/ai-cognitive-workflow.png[AI and human cognitive workflow]

Minimum event stream: change IDs · model/prompt versions · context inspected · finding type/severity/evidence · human views and expansions · suggestion applications · comment replies · downstream edits · reviewer duplication · quality outcomes · cost and latency.

*Interactions, not chain-of-thought.*

// ============================================================================

== Worked example: the $174k meeting cost

Eight engineers · 3.4 context switches/day · 11.5 min recovery · $145/hr loaded:

[stem]
++++
C_{recovery} = 3.4 \times \frac{11.5}{60} \times 145 \approx 94.5\ \text{USD/day}
++++

* Team: $756/day · *~$174k/year* in pure recovery overhead
* Consolidate two meetings → 2.1 switches/day, 10.8 min recovery
* New cost: ~$100.8k/year — *$73k saved*

The economic argument becomes visible instead of anecdotal.

// ============================================================================

== Ethical constraints are measurement requirements

Cognitive telemetry can easily become surveillance. Safeguards are non-negotiable:

* collect the minimum data for the decision
* prefer artifact and workflow events over keystrokes
* aggregate by system, change class, or team
* *prohibit* unsupported individual productivity scoring
* document purpose, retention, access, downstream use
* separate research telemetry from performance management

[.lead]
People who fear telemetry will alter behavior — and destroy the validity of the system.

// ============================================================================

== Key takeaways

* Human attention is the binding constraint in software delivery
* Cognitive burden is multidimensional — preserve the components
* Context switching creates recoverable, quantifiable cost
* Review burden depends on semantics and architecture, not size
* AI creates both *offload* and *reload* — measure the net
* Acceptance rate alone does not measure AI value
* Proxies require predictive, convergent, discriminant, and experimental validation
* Trust and ethics are prerequisites for valid measurement
